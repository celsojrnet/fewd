Welcome to ACME Corp!

The company name in the Road Runner cartoons is ironic, since the word acme is derived from the Greek origin - English translation meaning the peak, zenith or prime. Products from the fictional Acme Corporation are both generic and failure-prone. The name Acme became popular for businesses by the 1920s, when alphabetized business telephone directories such as the Yellow Pages began to be widespread. There were a flood of businesses named Acme (some of these still survive). For example, early Sears catalogues contained a number of products with the "Acme" trademark, including anvils, which are frequently used in Warner Bros., cartoons.
